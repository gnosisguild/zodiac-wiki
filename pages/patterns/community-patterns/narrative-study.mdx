# Narrative/Study

Primer article on Mumford and Ocalan: ["Hierarchy in the UK, Anarchy in Kurdistan"](https://www.bbc.co.uk/blogs/adamcurtis/entries/5a7b18b5-0ec3-3d3e-a307-54820a7c6a59) by Adam Curtis

Our philosophy is influenced by a combination of Mumford (philosophy of technology), Ocalan (political philosophy) and Werner (economic philosophy).

## Primary phase

### Instructions:

1. Study each material below
2. Make notes summarizing on each piece
3. Your own thoughts and critique
4. Report that will serve as a basis for a media piece connecting these concepts together (keep track of material since this will be referenced later)

### Materials:

- ["Authoritarian vs Democratic Technics"](https://www.jstor.org/stable/3101118) by Lewis Mumford
- Introduction to ["New Paradigm on Macroeconomics"](https://b-ok.cc/book/965553/95311d) by Richard Werner
    - Also see [original article](https://professorwerner.org/category/articles-essays/).
- Pages 64-74 of ["Manifesto for a Democratic Civilization part 2"](https://b-ok.xyz/book/23257210/9625b9) by Abdullah Ocalan
    - [IDEOLOGY document](https://github.com/darkrenaissance/darkfi/blob/master/IDEOLOGY.md) which contains an excerpt from Ocalan's third book on Democratic Civilization.
