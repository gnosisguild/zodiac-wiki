Narrative/Study

Primer article on Mumford and Ocalan: "Hierarchy in the UK, Anarchy in Kurdistan" by Adam Curtis

Our philosophy is influenced by a combination of Mumford (philosophy of technology), Ocalan (political philosophy) and Werner (economic philosophy).

Primary phase[edit]

Instructions:[edit]

  1. Study each material below
  2. Make notes summarizing on each piece
  3. Your own thoughts and critique
  4. Report that will serve as a basis for a media piece connecting these concepts together (keep track of material since this will be referenced later)

Materials:[edit]

  • "Authoritarian vs Democratic Technics" by Lewis Mumford
  • Introduction to "New Paradigm on Macroeconomics" by Richard Werner
    • Also see orginal article.
  • Pages 64-74 of "Manifesto for a Democratic Civilization part 2" by Abdullah Ocalan
    • IDEOLOGY document which contains an excerpt from Ocalan's third book on Democratic Civilization.

Second phase[edit]

  • Complete reading of "Manifesto for a Democratic Civilization part 2"
  • Produce report we can share which explains all these ideas in the areas of politics, economy and society.
  • Explain these concepts:
    • Civilization
    • Democracy
    • State
    • Capitalism
    • Liberalism
    • Nationalism
    • Modernity
    • Nation
    • Capitalist modernity
    • Democratic modernity
    • Democratic nation

Complementary[edit]

  • Ocalan on Science
  • Konkin, The New Libertarian Manifesto
  • The Zen of Python
  • Basics of Unix Philosophy
  • Do Artifacts Have Politics, Langdon Winner
